About Me

Hi there, I'm Jamie. I'm a front end web developer, travel, sports and finance enthusiast. I love tackling the problems and challenges that web development presents to be able to create responsive and user friendly websites. I'm always curious about learning new programming concepts to stay up to date with the most current tech being used, which is was what I feel I was missing in my previous career in the financial industry.

This is what led me to HackerYou and their Web Development Bootcamp. Being challenged each day and learning something new has been an amazing experience.


Outside of coding, you can find me doing some kind of physical activity, travelling to somewhere I haven't been to, and finding a place to enjoy sangrias.
